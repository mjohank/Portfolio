Who am I?

I am a 45 year old lad with a passion for transformation and technology. Hailing from sunny California, USA, I spent my formative years in Bergen before settling in the Haugesund area. For the past two decades, I’ve dedicated myself to a career as a correctional officer, but my curiosity for computers and technology has always burned bright in the background.

After soul-searching and seeking a fresh challenge, I decided to pivot towards frontend development. It’s a field that encapsulates my fascination with technology and blends seamlessly with the creative and innovative side of me. Currently, I’m pursuing my dreams with part-time studies at Noroff School of Technology, eager to carve a new path in this dynamic domain.

What can I do?

  • I specialize in crafting tailor-made web designs that seamlessly blend creativity with functionality. With a strong focus on accessibility, I ensure that all users can navigate and interact with the content effortlessly.
  • With a keen eye for detail and user experience, I ensure intuitive navigation and visually captivating interfaces to engage audiences effectively.
  • My expertise extends to optimizing website performance through responsive design techniques, ensuring compatibility across various devices for an enhanced user experience.
